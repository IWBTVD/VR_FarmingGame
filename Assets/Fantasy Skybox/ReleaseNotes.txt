		• Unity 2019.3.0f1 or higher.

		• Removed Howto folders and use Manual.chm file for manual.
		• Updated Sun/Sunless of 01A_Day, 01B_Day, 02A_Day, 02B_Day, 03A_Day, 03B_Day, 04A_Day, 04B_Day, 05A_Day, 05B_Day, 06A_Day, 06B_Day skyboxes.

		• Updated, uses FPSController of Unity's Standard Assets instead of GE's FPS Player.
		• 'GE Touch Joystick' in removed from 'GE Common Assets' folder.
		• Update demo scene and sample scripts.
